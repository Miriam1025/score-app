# Sourdough Recipe

## Overview

This is the base recipe used for default values in the app. User follows this workflow but may adjust quantities per bake.

---

## Stage 1: Starter Build
**Duration:** Overnight

In a clean jar, ADD (in grams):
| Ingredient | Default | Notes |
|------------|---------|-------|
| Starter | 50g | From fridge |
| Water | 50g | Room temp OR warmed (1 min microwave) |
| Flour | 50g | |

**Instructions:**
1. STIR water until no clumps
2. STIR flour until no dry spots
3. COVER top loosely
4. LEAVE somewhere warm overnight

**Ratio:** 1:1:1 (starter:water:flour)

**Target:** 2-3x rise by morning

---

## Stage 2: Mix Dough
**Duration:** 5-8+ hours

In a bowl, ADD (in grams):
| Ingredient | Default | Notes |
|------------|---------|-------|
| Water | 350g | Room temp OR warmed |
| Starter | 50g | From Stage 1, at peak |
| Flour | 500g | |
| Salt | 15g | |

**Instructions:**
1. ADD water
2. ADD starter, WHISK until foamy & no clumps
3. ADD flour, MIX until you feel no dry clumps
4. ADD salt, MIX again
5. COVER & let rest [30 minutes]

**Stretch & Fold:**
- COMPLETE stretch & fold set
- REPEAT every 30 min
- Duration: [2-3 hours]

**Bulk Fermentation:**
- LEAVE covered on the counter until almost doubled
- Duration: [2-5+ hours]

**Then:**
- SHAPE the dough
- PUT in a banneton bowl
- COVER with plastic wrap

**Target:** Dough approximately doubled

---

## Stage 3: Cold Proof
**Duration:** 6-48 hours

**Instructions:**
1. PUT in the fridge
2. Duration: [6-48 hours]

**Notes:**
- Longer cold proof = more sour flavor
- Can bake anytime within this window

## Stage 5: Rest
**Duration:** ~2 hours

**Instructions:**
- WAIT to cut (best practice)
- Duration: [~2 hours]

**Notes:**
- Cutting too early releases steam and affects texture
- Internal baking continues during rest
